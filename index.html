A simple, community-centric Minecraft SMP server!


  • A community SMP that was founded in August of 2019, aiming to create a fun, cozy, and relaxing place to play Minecraft!
  • Simplicity is our central focus! Our simple rules prohibit griefing and protect your builds from disaster.
  • Our economy is player-driven- players can create their own shops and warps, auction off items at their own prices, and trade with other players.
  • We have a gigantic map, with lots of space for everybody to build and explore! Our 80,000 x 80,000 block map is interconnected by a full library of player warps!
  • We stay up to date! Come join us at firestartermc.com with Minecraft 1.20: The Trails & Tales Update.
