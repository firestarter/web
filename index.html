A simple, community-centric Minecraft SMP!


  • A community-driven SMP that was founded in August of 2019, aiming to create a fun, chill, and relaxing place to play Minecraft!
  • Simplicity is central focus! Our simple rules prohibit griefing and protect your builds from disaster.
  • Our economy is player-driven- players can create their own shops and warps, auction off items at their own prices, and trade with other players.
  • We're currently running Minecraft 1.20, Trails & Tales! Come join us at firestartermc.com.
